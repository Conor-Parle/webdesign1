Take Me There!



Information

BSc (Hons) in Sports and Exercise Science is one of the best courses SETU Waterford has to offer. The course lasts 4 years and provides you with the opportunity to go on work placement in year 3. More informaton on the work placement can be found under the work placement heading in the "Menu" section. This course is the perfect course to choose if you aren't sure about what you want to do but sports is a big interest. This is because it's a general course so it can lead to loads of different careers. If you've always liked the idea of studying abroad, this course allows students to do that instead of work placement in year 3. The Tourism and Leisure Building is where you will spend most of your time on this course, this building is widely known as the most advanced building SETU Waterford has to offer. For many of the modules you will find yourself in the gym or outdoors at some point throughout, so if being stuck in a classroom all day isn't for you, this course is perfect.
